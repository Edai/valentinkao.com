"The Cognitive Dark Forest" hit **#1** on Hacker News today, 361 points.

The thesis: AI platforms learn from everything you publish. Your innovation becomes their median. Share or hide, you lose either way.  

You can read it here directly : https://ryelang.org/blog/posts/cognitive-dark-forest

AI can absorb the code, the way of writing, etc. It cannot absorb the trust or the image you built by sharing it. The reason to share changed. It didn't disappear.

A friend of mine, physics PhD researcher, told me he uses AI to rephrase his articles. The ideas are his. He just struggled to get them across clearly. I get that.
